How to cook ramen:

Why you should cook ramen:

Ramen is easy, it is tasty, it is cheap. Do I need to say more? Truly the unltimate food!

Ingredientes:

  • Ramen
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil and add some salt to water
  2. Add in the ramen and the seasoning package
  3. Wait for 3 minutes
  4. Drain the water
  5. Enjoy your ramen
